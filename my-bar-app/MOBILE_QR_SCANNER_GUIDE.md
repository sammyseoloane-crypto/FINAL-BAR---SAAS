# Mobile QR Scanner Guide for Staff

## Quick Start

1. **Open the app** on your mobile device (phone or tablet)
2. **Navigate to** QR Scanner from the staff menu
3. **Allow camera access** when prompted
4. **Start scanning** customer QR codes

## Mobile Features

### ✅ Optimized for Mobile Devices

- **Large scanning area** - 80% of screen for easy targeting
- **Auto-focus** - Automatically focuses on QR codes
- **Haptic feedback** - Phone vibrates when code is scanned
- **Flashlight support** - Use flash in low light (if supported)
- **Back camera priority** - Automatically selects rear camera on phones
- **Responsive design** - Works on all screen sizes

### 📱 How to Scan on Mobile

1. Click **"📸 Start Scanning"** button
2. Point your camera at the customer's QR code
3. Keep the QR code within the colored frame
4. Wait for vibration feedback (scan successful)
5. View results immediately on screen

### 🔦 Using the Flashlight

- If your device supports it, a flashlight/torch button will appear in the scanner
- Click it to toggle the flashlight on/off for scanning in dark environments

### 📊 Scan Results

After scanning, you'll see:

- **✅ Green** - Valid code, access granted
- **⚠️ Yellow** - Already scanned or payment pending
- **❌ Red** - Invalid code or error

### 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Hold steady** - Keep camera stable for 1-2 seconds
2. **Good lighting** - Ensure QR code is well-lit
3. **Proper distance** - Hold phone 6-12 inches from QR code
4. **Clean lens** - Wipe camera lens if blurry
5. **Full screen** - Don't minimize or switch apps while scanning

### 🚨 Troubleshooting

**Camera not working?**
- Check browser permissions for camera access
- On iPhone: Settings > Safari > Camera
- On Android: Settings > Apps > [Browser] > Permissions > Camera

**Can't scan QR code?**
- Increase brightness on customer's phone
- Ask customer to zoom in on QR code
- Try manual code entry if available

**Scanner is slow?**
- Close other browser tabs
- Restart browser
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache

### 📱 Browser Compatibility

Works best on:
- **iOS Safari** (iOS 14+)
- **Chrome** (Android 7+)
- **Samsung Internet**
- **Firefox Mobile**

### 🔐 Privacy & Security

- Camera is only active when scanning
- No images are stored or uploaded
- Camera access can be revoked anytime in browser settings
- All scanning happens locally on your device

### 2.2 Processing Limitation (Section 9-12)

**2.2.1 Lawfulness of Processing (Section 9)**

We process personal information lawfully:
- With your consent
- To fulfill contractual obligations
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect legitimate interests
- For public interest purposes

**2.2.2 Minimality (Section 10)**

We collect only information that is:
- Adequate for the purpose
- Relevant to the purpose
- Not excessive for the purpose

**What We Collect:**
- Name, email, phone (account management)
- Transaction data (service delivery)
- Usage data (service improvement)

**What We Do NOT Collect (unless necessary):**
- ID numbers (except for legal verification)
- Physical addresses (except for delivery)
- Biometric data
- Health information
- Criminal history

**2.2.3 Consent, Justification, Objection (Section 11)**

**When We Require Consent:**
- Marketing communications
- Optional analytics and tracking
- Social media profile connections
- Sharing data with third parties (beyond service delivery)

**How We Obtain Consent:**
- Clear, plain language explanations
- Voluntary and specific consent
- Documented consent records
- Easy withdrawal mechanism

**How to Withdraw Consent:**
- Account settings → Privacy preferences
- Email: privacy@nightclub.app
- Phone: [Phone Number]

**2.2.4 Collection Directly from Data Subject (Section 12)**

We collect information directly from you when possible:
- During account registration
- When making purchases or bookings
- Through customer support interactions
- Via voluntary surveys and feedback

**Exceptions (When We Collect from Third Parties):**
- Payment processors (Stripe) - transaction confirmation
- Social media platforms - if you connect your profile
- Public sources - venue reviews and ratings

**We Will Notify You:** If we collect information from third parties

### 2.3 Purpose Specification (Section 13-14)

**2.3.1 Purpose Specification (Section 13)**

We collect and process information for specific purposes:

| Purpose | Legal Basis | Data Used |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Account management | Contract | Name, email, password |
| Payment processing | Contract | Transaction details, payment method |
| Table reservations | Contract | Name, booking details, payment |
| Customer support | Legitimate interest | Contact info, issue description |
| Fraud prevention | Legitimate interest | IP, device info, transaction patterns |
| Marketing communications | Consent | Email, preferences |
| Service improvement | Legitimate interest | Usage analytics, feedback |

**2.3.2 Further Processing Limitation (Section 14)**

We do not process information for purposes incompatible with the original purpose without:
- Obtaining your consent
- Notifying you of the new purpose
- Ensuring the new purpose is related to the original

### 2.4 Further Processing Limitation (Section 15)

**Compatible Processing:**
- Purposes reasonably expected by you
- Purposes authorized by law
- Purposes necessary for historical/statistical research

**Incompatible Processing:**
We will NOT use your data for unrelated purposes without consent, such as:
- Selling data to third-party marketers
- Profiling for purposes unrelated to our Service
- Sharing with unrelated third parties

### 2.5 Information Quality (Section 16)

**2.5.1 Data Accuracy**

We ensure personal information is:
- Complete and not misleading
- Accurate and up-to-date
- Relevant to the purpose

**How We Maintain Accuracy:**
- Verification during account registration
- Regular prompts to update information
- Immediate correction upon request
- Automated validation checks

**How You Can Update Data:**
- Account settings → Profile information
- Contact support@nightclub.app

**2.5.2 Correction and Deletion**

You have the right to:
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of information (subject to legal obligations)
- Request restriction of processing

**Response Time:** 30 days from request

### 2.6 Openness (Section 17-18)

**2.6.1 Openness (Section 17)**

We are transparent about data processing:
- Publish Privacy Policy at https://nightclub.app/privacy-policy
- Provide POPIA compliance documentation
- Make Information Officer contact details available
- Respond to information requests

**2.6.2 Notification to Data Subject (Section 18)**

**At Collection, We Notify You Of:**
- Information being collected
- Name and address of responsible party

- Consequences of not providing information

**Notification Methods:**

### 2.7 Security Safeguards (Section 19)

**2.7.1 Technical Measures**

- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt), optional MFA
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access (RBAC)
- **API Security**: Rate limiting, authentication tokens
- **Monitoring**: Real-time security event logging (Sentry)
- **Backups**: Daily encrypted backups with 30-day retention

**2.7.2 Organizational Measures**

- **Staff Training**: All employees trained on POPIA compliance
- **Access Restrictions**: Need-to-know basis for data access
- **Confidentiality Agreements**: All staff sign NDAs
- **Incident Response Plan**: Documented breach response procedures
- **Vendor Management**: Security assessments for third parties
- **Regular Audits**: Annual POPIA compliance audits

**2.7.3 Breach Notification**

In the event of a data breach:
- **Immediate Action**: Contain breach within 1 hour
- **Investigation**: Assess scope within 24 hours
- **Notification to Information Regulator**: As soon as reasonably possible
- **Notification to Data Subjects**: Without undue delay (if substantial harm possible)
- **Documentation**: Record breach details, response, and remediation

**Report Suspected Breaches:** security@nightclub.app

### 2.8 Data Subject Participation (Section 23-25)

**2.8.1 Right of Access (Section 23)**

You have the right to:
- Request confirmation of personal information we hold
- Request a description of the information
- Request copies of your personal information
- Know the identity of third parties with access to your information

**How to Request Access:**
- Email: information-officer@nightclub.app
- Subject: "POPIA Access Request"
- Include: Full name, account email, identification (to verify identity)

**Response Time:** 30 days (extendable by 30 days for complex requests)

**Fee:** May be charged for excessive or repetitive requests (reasonable amount to recover costs)

**Format:** Electronic copy (JSON, CSV) or PDF report

**2.8.2 Correction or Deletion (Section 24)**

You have the right to:
- Request correction of inaccurate or misleading information
- Request destruction or deletion of information
- Request blocking of information from further processing

**Exceptions (We May Refuse Deletion):**
- Legal obligations require retention (e.g., financial records - 7 years)
- Information needed for legal claims
- Public interest purposes
- Archiving purposes

**Retention Periods:**
| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Financial records | 7 years | Tax law compliance |
| Active accounts | Indefinite | Service delivery |
| Inactive accounts | 24 months | Legitimate interest |
| Deleted accounts | 30 days | Recovery period |
| Backups | 90 days | Business continuity |

**How to Request Correction/Deletion:**
- Account settings → Delete account
- Email: information-officer@nightclub.app
- Phone: [Phone Number]

**2.8.3 Objection (Section 11(3))**

You may object to processing:
- On reasonable grounds related to your situation
- For direct marketing purposes (immediately honored)

**How to Object:**
- Email: information-officer@nightclub.app
- Marketing opt-out: Unsubscribe link in emails
- Account settings → Marketing preferences

**Our Response:**

## 3. Special Personal Information (Sections 26-35)

### 3.1 Categories of Special Personal Information

POPIA defines special personal information as:
- Religious or philosophical beliefs
- Race or ethnic origin
- Trade union membership
- Political persuasion
- Health or sex life
- Biometric information
- Criminal behavior

### 3.2 Our Processing of Special Personal Information

**We Do NOT Process Special Personal Information** except:
- Age verification (18+ for alcohol service) - legal requirement
- Criminal record checks for staff (if legally required)

**When We Process Special Information:**
- Obtain explicit consent
- Process only to the extent necessary
- Implement heightened security measures
- Limit access to authorized personnel only

### 3.3 Children's Personal Information (Section 35)

- Our Service is not directed at children under 18
- We do not knowingly collect information from children
- If we discover child data, we delete it immediately
- Consent from parent/guardian required for processing child data (if applicable)

## 6. Cross-Border Information Transfers (Section 72)

### 6.1 Transfers Outside South Africa

Your personal information may be transferred to:
- **European Union** (Supabase servers - adequate protection)
- **United States** (Stripe, Netlify, AWS - contractual protections)

### 6.2 Transfer Requirements

We ensure lawful transfers through:
- **Adequate Level of Protection**: Recipient country has adequate data protection laws (EU)
- **Consent**: You consent to the transfer
- **Contractual Protection**: Standard contractual clauses with operators
- **Necessary for Performance**: Transfer necessary for our contract with you
- **Legitimate Interests**: Transfer balances our interests with your rights

### 6.3 Information Regulator Approval

Transfers comply with Information Regulator's requirements and do not require individual approval where:
- EU recipients are subject to GDPR (adequate protection)
- US recipients have signed Standard Contractual Clauses
- Transfers are necessary for service delivery

Under GDPR, we process your data based on:

- **Consent**: You have given clear consent (e.g., marketing emails)

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data retained for 24 months after last login
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data retained for 30 days (recovery period)
- **Financial Records**: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Backups**: Retained for 90 days
- **Anonymized Analytics**: Retained indefinitely

### 6.2 Data Deletion

You can request data deletion at any time. Upon deletion:
- Personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
- Anonymized data may be retained for analytics
- Financial records are retained as required by law

## 7. Your Data Protection Rights

### 7.1 Rights Under GDPR (EU Users)

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Data Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for marketing or optional features
- **Lodge a Complaint**: File a complaint with your data protection authority

### 7.2 Rights Under POPIA (South Africa Users)

You have the right to:

- **Request Access**: Access personal information we hold about you
- **Request Correction**: Correct or delete inaccurate information
- **Object to Processing**: Object to certain uses of your information
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for optional processing
- **Lodge a Complaint**: File a complaint with the Information Regulator

### 7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Email: privacy@nightclub.app  
Subject: Data Rights Request  
Include: Your account email and specific request

We will respond within:
- **GDPR**: 30 days
- **POPIA**: 30 days
- **Other**: 45 days

### 8.1 Security Measures

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS)

**Organizational Safeguards:**

- Access controls and role-based permissions

**Monitoring:**

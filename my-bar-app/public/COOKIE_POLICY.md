## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your experience more convenient and the site more useful.

### 2.1 Types of Cookies

**Session Cookies:**
- Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- Used for authentication and maintaining your session

**Persistent Cookies:**
- Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
- Used to remember your preferences and settings

**First-Party Cookies:**
- Set by our website directly
- Used for core functionality and analytics

**Third-Party Cookies:**
- Set by external services (e.g., analytics, payment processors)
- Used for analytics, advertising, and service delivery

## 4. Similar Technologies

**localStorage Items:**

## 5. How to Manage Cookies

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Manager

When you first visit our Service, you'll see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject optional cookies (analytics, functionality)
- Customize cookie preferences

**To Change Preferences:**
- Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer
- Adjust your preferences
- Save changes

### 5.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Google Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose: Allow all cookies / Block third-party cookies / Block all cookies
3. Manage exceptions for specific sites

**Mozilla Firefox:**
1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose: Standard / Strict / Custom tracking protection
3. Manage exceptions

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy
2. Choose: Block all cookies / Prevent cross-site tracking
3. Manage website data

**Microsoft Edge:**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Choose: Allow all / Block third-party / Block all
3. Manage and delete cookies

**Mobile Browsers:**
- iOS Safari: Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security → Block All Cookies
- Chrome (Android): Settings → Site settings → Cookies → Block/Allow

### 5.3 Opt-Out Links

**Google Analytics Opt-Out:**
- Install: [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

**Do Not Track (DNT):**
- We respect DNT signals
- When DNT is enabled, we disable optional analytics cookies

### 5.4 Cookie Deletion

**To Delete Existing Cookies:**
1. Browser settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
2. Select: Cookies and other site data
3. Choose time range (last hour, day, all time)
4. Click "Clear data"

**Warning:** Deleting cookies may:
- Log you out of your account
- Reset your preferences
- Clear your shopping cart
- Affect Service functionality

- ❌ Cannot process payments
- ❌ Cannot make reservations

**Recommendation:** Do not block strictly necessary cookies

### 7.2 Analytics Cookies

If you block analytics cookies:
- ✓ Service still functions normally
- ⚠️ We cannot improve user experience based on usage data

**Impact:** Minimal impact on functionality

- ⚠️ Less convenient experience

**Impact:** Reduced convenience, but functional

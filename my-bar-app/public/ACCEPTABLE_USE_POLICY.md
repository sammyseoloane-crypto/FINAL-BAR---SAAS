# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** March 14, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the Nightclub Management Platform ("Service", "Platform", "we", "us", "our"). This policy is part of our [Terms of Service](./TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md).

By using our Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violation may result in account suspension or termination.

## 2. Acceptable Use

You may use our Service to:
- ✅ Manage your nightclub, bar, or venue operations
- ✅ Process legitimate customer transactions
- ✅ Make table reservations and bookings
- ✅ Order bottle service and bar items
- ✅ Manage guest lists and promoter activities
- ✅ Track sales and generate reports
- ✅ Communicate with customers and staff
- ✅ Access analytics and business insights
- ✅ Process payments securely
- ✅ Validate product authenticity via QR codes

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

You may NOT use the Service to:
- ❌ Engage in any illegal activity
- ❌ Violate any local, state, national, or international law or regulation
- ❌ Sell illegal substances or contraband
- ❌ Facilitate illegal gambling or betting
- ❌ Launder money or engage in financial fraud
- ❌ Violate alcohol licensing laws or age restrictions
- ❌ Evade taxes or violate financial reporting requirements
- ❌ Infringe upon intellectual property rights
- ❌ Violate privacy laws (GDPR, POPIA, CCPA, etc.)
- ❌ Engage in human trafficking or exploitation

### 3.2 Fraudulent Activities

You may NOT:
- ❌ Process fraudulent transactions
- ❌ Use stolen credit cards or payment methods
- ❌ Create fake reservations or bookings
- ❌ Manipulate pricing or overcharge customers
- ❌ Generate fake QR codes or counterfeit products
- ❌ Falsify sales reports or financial data
- ❌ Engage in chargebackfraud
- ❌ Impersonate other users or venues
- ❌ Use false identity or provide false information
- ❌ Create multiple accounts to abuse promotions

### 3.3 Security Violations

You may NOT:
- ❌ Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service
- ❌ Access another user's account without permission
- ❌ Bypass or circumvent security measures
- ❌ Exploit vulnerabilities in the Service
- ❌ Conduct security testing without written permission
- ❌ Use automated tools to scrape or extract data
- ❌ Launch denial-of-service (DoS) or DDoS attacks
- ❌ Introduce viruses, malware, or malicious code
- ❌ Intercept or monitor communications
- ❌ Reverse engineer or decompile the Service

### 3.4 Abuse of Service

You may NOT:
- ❌ Use the Service in a way that disrupts or degrades performance
- ❌ Overload systems with excessive API requests
- ❌ Create an unreasonable burden on infrastructure
- ❌ Use the Service for purposes other than intended
- ❌ Resell or redistribute the Service without authorization
- ❌ Create derivative works based on the Service
- ❌ Remove or obscure branding, trademarks, or copyright notices
- ❌ Use the Service to compete with us
- ❌ Benchmark the Service against competitors without permission

### 3.5 Harmful Content

You may NOT upload, post, or transmit content that is:
- ❌ Threatening, abusive, harassing, or hateful
- ❌ Defamatory or libelous
- ❌ Pornographic or sexually explicit (except age-appropriate venue marketing)
- ❌ Violent or graphic
- ❌ Discriminatory based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or national origin
- ❌ Inciting violence or criminal activity
- ❌ Promoting self-harm or suicide
- ❌ Containing personal information of others without consent
- ❌ Spam or unsolicited communications

### 3.6 Privacy Violations

You may NOT:
- ❌ Collect personal information from users without consent
- ❌ Share customer data with unauthorized third parties
- ❌ Use customer data for purposes other than intended
- ❌ Sell customer email addresses or phone numbers
- ❌ Send spam or unsolicited marketing messages
- ❌ Scrape user profiles or data
- ❌ Track users without their knowledge or consent
- ❌ Violate GDPR, POPIA, CCPA, or other privacy laws

### 3.7 Intellectual Property Violations

You may NOT:
- ❌ Use copyrighted material without permission
- ❌ Upload pirated music, videos, or software
- ❌ Use trademarks or logos without authorization
- ❌ Copy or plagiarize content from other sources
- ❌ Claim ownership of our intellectual property
- ❌ Remove attribution or copyright notices

### 3.8 Interference with Others

You may NOT:
- ❌ Interfere with other users' access or enjoyment of the Service
- ❌ Harass, stalk, or threaten other users
- ❌ Impersonate staff, administrators, or other users
- ❌ Create or distribute misinformation about venues or users
- ❌ Leave fake or defamatory reviews
- ❌ Manipulate ratings or reviews
- ❌ Coordinate attacks against specific users or venues

Venues must:
- ✅ Hold valid liquor licenses

### 5.2 Financial Compliance

- ✅ Report all sales accurately
- ✅ Comply with tax laws and regulations
- ✅ Maintain accurate financial records

- ✅ Refund customers as appropriate

Venues may NOT:
- ❌ Underreport sales to evade taxes

- ❌ Manipulate transaction records
- ❌ Engage in money laundering

Venue owners/managers must:
- ✅ Create accounts only for legitimate employees

- ✅ Monitor staff activities for compliance

Venue owners are responsible for:
- All actions taken by staff under their accounts
- Unauthorized access by former employees
- Misuse of the Service by their staff
